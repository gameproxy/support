About

GameProxy is a website where you can play official games uploaded by GameProxy from multiple verified and stated sources. If you are a game publisher and want to get your game published or have any copyright concerns, please click here.

You can comment on games, see how to play them, go full screen, read the description, see who developed/shared the game and see other games. On the home page you can see recommended games and on the chat tab you can talk to other users about games. You can also create group chats and talk in the game chat (this is the same as the comments description).

We have retro games, unblocked-style games, scratch games, modern browsr games and others.
